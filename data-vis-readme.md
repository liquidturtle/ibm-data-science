Types of Plots
- Line plot
  - trends over time
  - compare datasets with a continuous independent variable (e.g. age or time)
  - cause and effect relationship (e.g. sales revenue based on marketing budget)
- Bar chart
  - compare different categories or groups
  - discrete data
  - data than can be ranked or ordered
- scatter plot
  - examine relationship between two continuous variables
  - investigate patterns or trends in data
  - detect outliers or unusual observations
